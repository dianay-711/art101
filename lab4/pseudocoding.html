Lab 4: Pseudocoding and Problem-solving

Challenge

The challenge for this lab was to understand pseudocoding and learn how to excecute it. Firstly, we came up with a simple everyday task, making a peanut butter and jelly sandwich, and wrote out the individual steps. After that, replicated this process but instead we came up with a computer task. The computer task was creating a game of Connect 4.

Problems

Initially, when I was working with my partner, we were a bit confused on how to structure the pseudocode. We were not communicating well and they started working with someone else. So tackling this lab on my own was a bit overwhelming and I felt lost at some points but I am pretty proud of how it turned out.

Results

Pseudocoding an Everyday Task: Making a PB&J


			//Gather your ingredients for the sandwich: Bread, Peanut Butter, Jelly
			//Gather materials: knife and plate
			//Pull out two slices of bread and place on the plate.
			//Open the peanut butter and jelly jars.
			//With the knife, spread the peanut butter onto one slice of bread.
			//Again using the knife, spread the jelly onto the other slice of bread.
			//Place the slices with the peanut butter and jelly on top of each other.
			//Place your completed PB&J on the plate and enjoy :)
